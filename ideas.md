# The Systemic Architect - Design Guide

## Brand Overview
**Business:** The Systemic Architect - AI compliance guidance for small businesses and solopreneurs  
**Tagline:** "AI compliance handled. Get on with business."  
**Voice:** Authoritative, plain-spoken, quietly warm. No corporate jargon, no hype.

## Design Philosophy: Boutique Consulting Firm Aesthetic

This website embodies the aesthetic of a boutique consulting firm—refined, trustworthy, and sophisticated. The design prioritizes:
- **Generous whitespace** for breathing room and elegance
- **Refined typography hierarchy** with clear visual structure
- **Intentional color usage** that feels premium, not overwhelming
- **Professional understated elegance** that instills trust
- **High-quality visual balance** throughout

## Typography

**Headings:** Merriweather (serif, elegant, authoritative)  
**Body Text:** Open Sans (sans-serif, clean, readable)

### Hierarchy
- **H1 (Hero Headline):** Large, commanding, Navy Ink
- **H2 (Section Headings):** Prominent, Navy Ink
- **Body Copy:** Open Sans, Navy Ink or Dark Denim for secondary text
- **Captions/Labels:** Dark Denim, smaller weight

## Page Structure

1. **Header:** Logo only, Warm Ivory background
2. **Hero Section:** Headline, subheadline, clean layout
3. **Value Points Section:** Bulleted list, clear and readable
4. **Newsletter Sign-up Section:** Heading, subheading, single email field form
5. **Payhip Products Section:** Heading, body copy, CTA button
6. **Footer:** Business name + tagline, contact email, social links, privacy policy placeholder

---

## Form Integration

The newsletter form is structured to be API-ready for Sender integration:
- Single email field (no name field)
- Button text: "Get The Systemic Shift"
- On submit message: "Good to have you here."
- Form structure allows for easy connection to Sender's API

---

## Responsive Design

- **Mobile-first approach** (60%+ of visitors on mobile)
- **Fully responsive** across all screen sizes
- **Touch-friendly** button and form sizes
- **Readable text** at all breakpoints

---

## Design Decisions

- **Whitespace:** Ample margins and padding create breathing room and sophistication
- **Typography:** Merriweather for headings provides elegance; Open Sans for body ensures readability
- **Color Restraint:** Limited color palette used strategically (not overwhelming)
- **Consistency:** All sections follow the same design language and spacing rhythm
- **Trust Signals:** Clean layout, professional typography, and careful color usage instill confidence
